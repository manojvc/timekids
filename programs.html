Programs Offered at TIME Kids Preschool Kazhakkoottam

Explore our Playgroup, Nursery, PP1, and PP2 programs — nurturing creativity, confidence, and early learning for young children near Technopark, Trivandrum.

Playgroup (2–3 Years)

Our Playgroup program introduces children to a world of exploration through sensory activities, social play, and joyful learning experiences.

Nursery (3–4 Years)

Building strong foundations in language, numeracy, motor skills, and social confidence through structured play and early academics.

PP1 (4–5 Years)

Encouraging early literacy, numeracy, problem-solving, and creativity — preparing children for formal learning with confidence and joy.

PP2 (5–6 Years)

Strengthening academic skills, communication, and critical thinking to ensure a smooth transition into primary school education.

Activity-Based Learning

Creative arts, music, storytelling, and games to foster imagination, collaboration, and self-expression in young learners.

Life Skills & Values

Fostering independence, empathy, communication skills, and emotional resilience in a safe and caring environment.
